Questions

Before you book

Do I need to buy detergent?

No — bring your own preferred detergent, or ask your Line Worker to supply hers for a small fee shown upfront in the app before you confirm.

What if I'm not home during the visit?

Most customers aren't. Leave entry instructions in the app, and you'll get a photo update when she arrives and another when she's finished.

How is a Line Worker's pay set?

Base pay per visit plus a neighborhood rate for basket size and driving distance, deposited weekly with no fees taken from tips.

Can I request the same Line Worker every time?

Yes — most households build a standing weekly route with one or two Line Workers they trust with their machines and their clothes.

What areas do you serve?

We're rolling out neighborhood by neighborhood. Enter your zip code in the app to see if your street is live yet.
